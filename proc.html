Supplier Management

The Supplier Management section is responsible for identifying, evaluating, and maintaining relationships with suppliers to ensure quality and timely delivery of goods and services.

Purchase Orders

The Purchase Orders section handles the creation, approval, and tracking of purchase orders to ensure that all procurement activities are documented and compliant with hospital policies.

Budgeting and Cost Control

The support for the cost-control legislation has come largely from people who have a professional or business interest in the subject: public administrators, economists, some of the insurers.

Quality Assurance

Ensures that all procured goods and services meet the hospital's quality standards and regulatory requirements.

Compliance and Ethics

The Compliance and Ethics section ensures all procurement activities adhere to legal and ethical standards, transparency and accountability.
