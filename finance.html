Home

Budgeting

Responsible for planning and allocating financial resources to various departments to ensure efficient operations.

Billing

Handles all patient billing inquiries, processes invoices, and ensures that payments are collected in a timely way.

Insurance

Manages relationships with insurance providers, verifies patient insurance coverage, and handles claims.

Financial Reporting

Prepares financial statements, monitors financial performance, and provides insights to support decision-making.

Payroll

Ensures that all hospital staff are compensated accurately and on time, managing payroll processing and tax compliance.

Financial Planning

The Financial Planning team develops long-term financial strategies, forecasts revenue, and assesses the financial viability of new projects.
